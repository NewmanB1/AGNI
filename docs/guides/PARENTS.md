# Guide: Parents

How to link to your child's learning account and view their progress.

---

## What you need

- A phone or computer with a web browser
- The hub URL from your child's school (e.g. `http://hub.local/` or an IP address)
- A **6-character invite code** from your child's teacher

---

## Step 1: Open the Parent Dashboard

Go to the hub URL in your browser and click **Parent Dashboard** on the home page, or navigate directly to `/parent/dashboard`.

---

## Step 2: Enter your name

Type any name or identifier for yourself (e.g. "Maria" or "Parent-Ahmed"). This is how the system recognizes you on this device. It is stored locally on your phone — it is not sent to the school.

---

## Step 3: Enter the invite code

Type the 6-character code your child's teacher gave you (e.g. `A3K7P2`). Click **Link**.

If the code is valid, you'll see a confirmation and your child will appear in your dashboard.

**Note:** Each code works once and expires after 7 days. If it doesn't work, ask the teacher for a new one.

---

## Step 4: View your child's progress

Click your child's name to see:

- **Skills mastered** — how many topics they've completed out of the total
- **Completion percentage** — overall progress
- **Recommended lessons** — what the system thinks they should do next
- **Teacher override** — if the teacher has manually assigned a specific lesson, it shows here

The progress bars show mastery for each individual skill. Green means mastered; partial fill means in progress.

---

## Multiple children

If you have more than one child using AGNI, ask the teacher for a separate invite code for each child. Enter each code on the same dashboard — all your children will appear as clickable cards.

---

## Privacy

- You see your child's learning data only — not other students'.
- Student names are shown as pseudonymous IDs (e.g. "px-a3f2b1"). The teacher assigns display names separately.
- No personal information (real names, photos, etc.) is stored in the system.
- Your parent identifier is stored only on your device.

---

## Troubleshooting

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| "Invalid or expired invite code" | Ask the teacher for a new code. Codes expire after 7 days. |
| Can't reach the hub | Make sure you're connected to the school's WiFi network. The hub only works on the local network. |
| Progress not updating | Progress updates when your child completes lessons. Check back later, or ask the teacher if lessons are being assigned. |
| "Not linked to this student" | You may have typed a different parent name than the one you used when entering the code. Use the **Switch** button to re-enter your name. |
